Types of Disasters

Disasters can be natural or man-made events that cause significant disruption, destruction, and distress to communities. Here are some common types:

  • Natural Disasters: Earthquakes, floods, hurricanes, and wildfires.
  • Technological Disasters: Chemical spills, nuclear accidents, and industrial explosions.
  • Humanitarian Crises: Conflicts, refugee crises, and pandemics.

Understanding these disasters is crucial for effective preparedness and response.
